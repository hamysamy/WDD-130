Street Spots

Discover the best street spots Rexburg has to offer! From ledges to stair sets, gaps to banks, this is where you’ll find the top street skating locations. Each spot includes difficulty rankings, photos, and skater-submitted tips.

KIWI LEDGE

The KIWI ledge is a perfect height and very smooth for grinds. The only problem is some cracks that make the run-up a little technical. There are usually a lot of people walking on the sidewalk, so remember to be respectful to everyone! It gets rough sometimes, so you will definitely want to bring wax for an ideal experience.

Pin

5-6-7

5-6-7 are three stair sets right next to each other, which are perfect for practice. If you want to raise your confidence in stair skating, you can slowly level up here. One thing to worry about is the people. If you go during the day, there can be many students walking. This spot might be better for intermediate to advanced skaters but is still a great spot to learn. It would be best to go when there aren’t many students, so at night or later in the day. The run-up is not the longest, but it gives you enough room to hit it without struggle.
